Professor.

A teacher for fifteen years, Jeremy Sarachan teaches courses in new media production and theory. His research covers pedagogical practices related to coding instruction and the ways in which children play creatively in virtual spaces. He directs the program in Digital Cultures and Technologies.

Adviser.

Professor Sarachan advises students in both Media and Communication and Digital Cultures and Technologies, providing opportunities to discuss both coursework and career planning.
